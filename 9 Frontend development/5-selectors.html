Pseudoclases and pseudoelements

Pseudoclasses or elements are modificatios of what a class will look like in certian cases.


  • Pseudoclass: When a class is under a speciall circunstance, like having the mouse over them or being the first of its class. The important part is that they act as if they were a new class applyied on a certian element.
  • Pseudoelements: They work very similar to pseudocalsses, but instead the act as if they were complete new HTML elements
